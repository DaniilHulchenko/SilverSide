Old House

Perched on the rocks of pristine Craig Pond, Craigmere is a classic shingle-style Maine lake house, built in 1900 and cherished for generations by the same family. The cottage features a magnificent wrap around screen porch overlooking the water, bathed in sunlight and surrounded by evergreens and mountains. Beautifully handcrafted from stem to stern this home features two wood burning fireplaces in the living and dining rooms and four charming bedrooms with exposed beams and windows to the pond. Currently off the grid and powered by a generator with water drawn from the pond.
